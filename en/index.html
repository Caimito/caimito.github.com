Regenerative Farm

Organizations can be created to either exploit or regenerate something. The intent of the organization's leadership determines the methods and techniques being used and defines the behavior of everyone working in the organization.

People with a regenerative mindset have come up with methods and techniques such as Agile Software Development, DevOps, Lean Manufacturing, Beyond Budgeting, Permaculture and Holistic Management - just to name a few.

Together with our farm team I build, operate and maintain an organic farming operation based on these principles in Andalucia, Spain.

We practice Regenerative Agriculture which means we design living systems that are able to regenerate themselves and thus provide a high yield with little or no external input.
