Available And Planned Seminars

As an introduction to several topics I offer half-day and full-day seminars in many locations across Europe in English, German, and Spanish. Please have a look at what is scheduled or planned. Or let me know what would be helpful for you and we find a way to make it happen in your location.

Currently I'm working on a concept for a workshop about Activity-Centered Design.

In general these events should be kept as affordable as possible. I am not a training vendor. My motivation for doing these events is to introduce people to new techniques, new concepts, new ways of doing their work. The exact price per participant depends on the number of participants, the venue, catering, and my own travel expenses. Of course, I'm more than happy to perform such an event for a private group.

In order to keep it affordable and make reservation of a good venue and travel arrangements easier, tickets for public events will be offered for an early bird price and a regular price. All tickets have to be paid in full a few days before the event takes place.
