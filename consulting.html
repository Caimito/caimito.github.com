Be agile vs. Do Agile

Adopting agile practices and processes like Scrum or Kanban is not the goal for a learning organization.

Instead we help our clients to become more agile by using an approach to coaching that itself is based on inspect-and-adapt. Depending on where you and your teams are at we start with the most simple practices that help to work more effective and add gradually other practices that fit and help to develop. Our approach is free of dogma so that your organization can become agile and not be doing Agile.
