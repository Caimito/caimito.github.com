Be agile vs. Do Agile

Adopting agile practices and processes like Scrum or Kanban is not the goal for a learning organization.

Instead we help our clients to become more agile by using an approach to coaching that itself is based on inspect-and-adapt. Depending on where you and your teams are at we start with the most simple practices that help to work more effective and add gradually other practices that fit and help to develop. Our approach is free of dogma so that your organization can become agile and not be doing Agile.

Humans are permanent learners. Nobody has to teach an infant to walk, speak and understand the world around them. Toddlers continue this in more complex domains. When we enter adulthood we continue learning. But we might learn the wrong things. We might learn to fear superiors and not serve the customer. We might learn to go with the flow instead of expressing the insight we just had. We might learn to climb up the corporate ladder quicker or what it takes to jump ship instead of how to collaborate better across boundaries in the organization for the benefit of the organization and its customers.

In a learning organization we re-learn to see the whole and connect the dots instead of just performing well in the silos we find ourselves in.
